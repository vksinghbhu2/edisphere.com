Electronic Data Interchange (EDI) in Chemical/Petroleum Industry

EDI technology is used in the Chemical/Petroleum industry to enable the sharing of business documents and greater productivity across the supply chain which comprises of Manufacturers, Suppliers, and Customers.

Examples of organizations using EDI in the Chemical/Petroleum Industry:-
Shell Oil Company, Atlantic Petroleum, Royal Dutch Shell, National Oil Corporation, Occidental Petroleum, Qatar Petroleum, Indian Oil Corporation, Iraq National Oil Company, Exxcon Mobil, Saudi Arabian Oil Company

Associations for EDI in the Chemical/Petroleum Industry:- PolyOne, Lyondell Barretts, Petroleum Industry Data Exchange (PIDX), CIDX (The Chemical Industry Data exchange)

Some of the popular EDI messages used in the Chemical/Petroleum industry include:-

Order related:-
 -Request For Quotation
-Response To Request For Quotation
-Purchase Order Messages
-Invoice
-Order Messages


Shipping related:-
-Advance Ship Notice
-Delievery Schedule
-Shipping Instructions
-Transportation Carrier Status Message


Finance related:-
-Credit/Debit Adjustment
-Payment Order Remittance Advice
-Price/Sales Catalogue
-Functional Acknowledgement

Popular Data Formats: X12, EDIFACT, XML, Proprietary formats.

Benefits of EDI in the Chemical/Petroleum Industry:-
1.Billing and Automated order processing is improved.
2.Better customer service.
3.Lower transaction cost
